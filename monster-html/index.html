INTRO

What is xucana ?

Xucana is a lovely hotel, in the heart of Danang, within the triangle formed by the bustling pedestrian shopping street Han utca, the fascinating Central Chợ đêm and the R Bridge. In addition to excellent transport links at the meeting point of a main metro line and tram line, our hotel is surrounded by the city centre’s elegant shops, cafés, restaurants, bars, the famous Gellert Thermal Spa, Szabadsag hid and other tourist sights just a few steps from the left bank of the Danube. Recently, our whole hotel has undergone a complete renovation.
